PROGRAM WHITE PAPER

AURNOVA University MSAI Program: White Paper and Technical Roadmap

Revolutionary AI-Powered Education for All Disciplines

AURNOVA University is launching a fully online Master of Science in Artificial Intelligence (MSAI) designed for working professionals from ANY academic background. This innovative program leverages artificial intelligence at every level – from curriculum development to course delivery and student support – to create a next-generation "AI-native" university experience that democratizes AI education.

Key Program Highlights:

  • Vision and Philosophy: A paradigm shift to an AI-driven learning ecosystem where AI is an epistemic partner in education rather than just a tool
  • Academic Framework: A robust curriculum of core courses, electives, and a thesis project ensures comprehensive coverage of AI fundamentals
  • AI-Driven Model: The program pioneers an AI-developed, AI-taught, AI-staffed, and AI-graded model
  • Co-Evolutionary Learning: Each student is paired with a personal AI assistant ("AurAI") that grows in knowledge and capability alongside them
  • Governance and Ethics: Despite heavy use of AI, the program maintains rigorous faculty oversight and governance

Executive Summary

AURNOVA's MSAI program represents a revolutionary approach to AI education, combining rigorous academic training with cutting-edge AI technology. Our co-evolutionary learning model pairs each student with a personal AI companion that grows alongside them throughout their educational journey.

  • Fully online program for working professionals
  • Accessible to students from ANY academic background
  • AI-developed, AI-taught, AI-staffed, and AI-graded
  • Personal AI companions (AurAI) for each student
  • Comprehensive thesis-based capstone project
  • SACSCOC and ABET accreditation alignment*
